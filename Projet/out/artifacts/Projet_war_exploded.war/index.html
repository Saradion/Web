The JEE Project: learning code while playing!

What is the JEE Project ?

This JEE Project aims to provide the (functional) template of a learning website about algorithms and programing. The twist? It does it by making you play games - called "challenges"-. Through its vast variety of challenge, everyone, beginners like pro, will be able to find something interesting. So what are you waiting for ? Come play with us now !
